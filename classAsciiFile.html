Detailed Description

Helps reading an ascii file.

Usage:

  • Open file. X.open("address")
  • Go to Beginning: X.gotobeg()
  • Finding: X.find("str")
  • NextLine: X()
  • Reading: X(var_to_read, "name to give warning")
  • Close: X.close()
